What You Can Do


Education

One of the most important things that you can do is to be educated on the topic at hand. Whether you're involved with the debates happening in court or simply talking to colleagues and friends, having accurate and current information is the best place to start.

Some good places to start include:

  • Tennessee State Legislation

    The Tennessee State Government keeps a website with all the information regarding legislation for public use. Each piece of legislation includes links to copies of the documents themselves as well as summaries and amendments to the laws for further reading. The site linked is a specific page for looking for legislation based on certain categories. A general search option is available on the top-right corner of the site.

    Learn More
  • Center for Reproductive Rights

    The Center for Reproductive Rights is a global human rights organization that works to ensure reproductive rights are protected in law as human rights. The Center keeps a website up to date with the latest information regarding reproductive rights around the world, with individual pages for specific areas, in a more readable format compared to the Tennessee Legislation site. Actions

Donate to Abortion Funds

Organizations like Abortion Care TN and Planned Parenthood often rely on donations for funding. Donating to these organizations allows them to continue providing abortion care for those seeking it.

Volunteer with Reproductive Rights Organizations

Many of those same organizations are also staffed by volunteers. By becoming a volunteer, you can help support those seeking abortion care. Planned Parenthood has a form you can fill out online for becoming a volunteer with them.

Contact Your Elected Officials

The Tennessee State Government has a site listing the current state legislators elected into office as well as their contact information. Email, call, or send a letter to your local representative sharing your beliefs on the situation and suggest things that you feel should be done.
